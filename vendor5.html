Artique

Artique, founded in 2021 by Rajesh Kumar in Pune, India, specializes in handcrafted decorative items like brass sculptures and painted clay figurines. The brand emphasizes sustainability, using eco-friendly materials and supporting local artisans with fair wages. Artique blends traditional craftsmanship with modern design, offering unique, culturally rich pieces for a global audience.
