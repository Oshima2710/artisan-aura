Craftsy

Craftsy Handicraft was founded in 2015 by Aarav Mehta in New Delhi, India, with the mission to preserve traditional craftsmanship and make it accessible globally. Starting with pottery and woodwork, the brand quickly expanded to include home décor, textiles, and jewelry. Committed to sustainability and fair trade, Craftsy Handicraft supports local artisans, offering unique, handmade products to customers worldwide.
