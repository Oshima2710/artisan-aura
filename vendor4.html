Tradify

Tradify, founded in 2019 by Meera Patel in Udaipur, India, specializes in blending traditional craftsmanship with modern design. Initially focused on hand-painted wooden artifacts, it now offers handwoven rugs, terracotta jewelry, and home decor. Committed to sustainability and fair trade, Craftsy supports local artisans while promoting eco-friendly practices and preserving cultural heritage.
